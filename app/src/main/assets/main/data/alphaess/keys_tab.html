Help: Key statistics

The top of the tab indicates the data range used to calculate self consumption and self sufficiency. The self sufficiency value considers what was used of the generated electricity (as opposed to the theoretical maximum use).

There are five controls:

Icon Purpose Details
Configure the step increment

The step size options are: day, week, month and year. Stepping relates only to the self consumption and self sufficiency figures.

Skip forward or backwards by the configured step interval. When the last day of a month is selected in the range, and the step is monthly, the last day of the month is preserved. Otherwise the to date will be preserved when stepping.
Select the date range to be rendered in the graph A standard date picker dialog to select a from and to date for the range. Date selection is inclusive.
Select the month to be displayed in the table Makes it easier to to multi-year comparisons of the same calendar month

In landscape mode, the zoom button to the top left may be used to show/hide the tabs and menu bar.
