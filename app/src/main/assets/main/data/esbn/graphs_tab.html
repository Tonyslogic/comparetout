The top of the tab indicates the data range rendered in the graph.

There are seven controls:

Icon Purpose Details
Configure the x-axis and step increment

The data may be rendered in groups of hours, weeks, days, months or years. To make it easier to change the selected date range, the next and previous buttons are configurable so you can align the grouping with the step size. Step options are: day, week, month and year.
Sum or average values for given range

Summing provides the totals of each selected data type for each hour/day/ month/year visible in the graph across the selected date range.

Averaging is the sum divided by the total selected range.

Months are summed separately based on year, but averaged across years.
Skip forward or backwards by the configured step interval. When the last day of a month is selected in the range, and the step is monthly, the last day of the month is preserved. Otherwise the to date will be preserved when stepping.
Select the date range to be rendered in the graph A standard date picker dialog to select a from and to date for the range. Date selection is inclusive.
Select the type of graph to display Toggles between one of four graph types: Bar; Line; Pie.
Select the date types to be rendered in the graph Shows or hides: Feed; Buy

In landscape mode, the zoom button to the top left may be used to show/hide the tabs and menu bar.
