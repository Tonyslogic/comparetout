Help: Filter button

Filter the comparison table. The table will automatically switch to landscape when more than five columns are selected

Rows can be filtered by:

  • Usage(s), usage what-if scenarios
  • Costs. Supplier and plan names
  • Net. The overall cost of the usage, cost combination
  • Buy. The total cost of electricity units purchased
  • Sell. The total income of electricity units exported
  • Bonus. Contract sign-up bonus (if any).
  • Fixed. The fixed costs of the supplier price plan
