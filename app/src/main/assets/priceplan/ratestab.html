Help: Costs rates tab

The cost defines a contract with a supplier. This is also referred to as a price plan

Use this tab to record the rate aspects of a supplier plan:

  • Date range. The range of dates for which the rates are applicable
  • Days. The days of the week for which the rates are applicable
  • Rates. The start hour, end hour and price per kWh

From this tab it is possible to:

  • Add a new rate (in edit mode, bottom of table)
  • Delete a rate (in edit mode, trashcan icon)
  • Edit the cost (supplier price plan), menu
  • Share the cost (supplier price plan), menu
  • Save the cost (supplier price plan), menu
  • Add a rates tab, menu
  • Delete a rates tab, menu
  • See why the cost is invalid, menu
