Help: Hourly tab

The hourly distribution tab covers electricity usage by hour of day.

There are two modes of operation for this tab: View; Edit.

  • View. Displays a bar chart of electricity usage by hour of day
  • Edit. Provides a variable number of input ranges. Ranges may be added or removed using the 'Split' and 'Del' buttons in each row. At most 24 ranges are allowed. Each range has a 'From' hour, a 'To' hour and the percentage of total electricity used in that period. You can edit the fields, or use the + and - buttons to increment and decrement respectively. The total is advisory, so don't get too hung up on ensuring it's exactly 100.
