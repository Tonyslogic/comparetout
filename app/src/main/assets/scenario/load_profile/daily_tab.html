Help: Daily tab

The daily distribution tab covers electricity usage by day of week.

There are two modes of operation for this tab: View; Edit.

  • View. Displays a bar chart of electricity usage by day of week
  • Edit. Provides 7 inputs, one for each day of the week. Each input is a percentage of total usage. You can edit the fields, or use the + and - buttons to increment and decrement respectively. The total is advisory, so don't get too hung up on ensuring it's exactly 100.
