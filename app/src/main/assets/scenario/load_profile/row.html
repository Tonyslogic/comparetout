Help: HDF costings table

Each row shows summary costs for a usage/cost combination.

Rows can show:

  • Supplier:Plan. Supplier and plan names
  • Net. The overall cost of the usage, cost combination
  • Buy. The total cost of electricity units purchased
  • Sell. The total income of electricity units exported
  • Fixed. The fixed costs of the supplier price plan

Clicking on a row will show a pie chart breakdown of the percentage of electricity purchased at each rate in the supplier plan (cost).
