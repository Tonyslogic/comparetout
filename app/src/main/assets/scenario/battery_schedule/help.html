Help: Battery charge scheduling

Once the battery is defined, it can be charged from the grid. This is a great cost saving option when the cost of electricity varies by time of day.

If you supplier price plan includes being paid for export, and export rate is greater than a off-peak or night rate, then it makes financial sense to charge the battery, and sell more excess generation.

In edit mode, charge schedules can be added (button in bottom right), and deleted (menu)

The battery charge schedule page has three areas:

AreaDescription
Connected Inverter nameUsed to identify the specific inverter. This is needed to ensure that the batteries are charged by the correct panel string first.
Days and monthsThe days of the week and months of the year on which the battery should be charged. All charge times listed share these days and months. Use the button to show/hide this area.
Charge timesMultiple charge times (From, to and the charge stop point -- a percentage of SOC) can be defined for each applicable day. Charge times can be added and removed. Linked charge times are indicated by an enabled link button.
