Help: EV divert schedules

Tutorial on YouTube

EV diverts may be scheduled at any time.

In edit mode, divert schedules can be added (button in bottom right), and deleted (menu)

The EV divert schedule tab has three areas: Properties; Applicable days and months; On/Off times.

AreaDescription
Properties
  • Name: A convenient way to refer to an EV divert schedule.
  • Active: Turn this schedule on or off.
  • EV diversion first: Charge the EV before diverting to water heating (if active).
  • Daily max diversion: The daily cut-off for EV diversion.
  • Minimum kW to divert: Diversion will only start when there is sufficient excess generation.
Use the button to show/hide this area.
Days and monthsThe days of the week and months of the year on which the EV should be charged. All charging times listed share these days and months. Use the button to show/hide this area.
Schedule timesMultiple scheduled times (From and to) can be defined for each applicable day. Scheduled times can be added and removed. Linked scheduled times are indicated by an enabled link button.
