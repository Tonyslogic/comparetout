Help: Water settings

Only a single hot water system may be configured in each usage what-if scenario.

The hot water settings has two areas: System properties; Daily usage

The system is described by six entries:

EntryDescription
HW heating capacityHow much water can the immersion heat? This will depend on the size of the tank and the position of the immersion in the tank. For example a 330l tank with the immersion half way up has a capacity of 165l. Of course if you have a de-stratification pump the capacity will be the same as the tank volume.
Intake temperature (째C)The temperature of the cold water being fed into the hot water tank.
Daily heat loss (째C)If no water is drawn, what is the temperature difference after 24 hours A factory insulated tank will lose up to 8째C. Loose lagging jackets more, and an uninsulated tank ,lots more.
Immersion rating (kWh)How much electricity will the immersion draw when turned on. The simulation for simplicity assumes all of this will result in water heating.
Target temperature (째C)At what temperature will the immersion shut off.
Daily usageHow much hot water will be used over the course of a day.

The daily usage area cover when in the day water is used, and how much of the daily usage (%) is consumed at that time. This allows for different usage patterns such as morning showers or bathing kids in the evening. Usage can be added or removed.
