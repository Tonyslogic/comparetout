Help: Hot water schedules

Hot water heating may be scheduled at any time.

In edit mode, schedules can be added (button in bottom right), and deleted (menu)

The hot water settings tab has two areas: Applicable days and months; On/Off times.

AreaDescription
Days and monthsThe days of the week and months of the year on which the water should be heated. All heating times listed share these days and months. Use the button to show/hide this area.
Schedule timesMultiple scheduled times (From and to) can be defined for each applicable day. Scheduled times can be added and removed. Linked scheduled times are indicated by an enabled link button.
