Help: Usage daily detail tab

There are three areas of interest in this tab:

  • Bar chart. Displays selected hourly statistics
  • Line chart. Displays cumulative battery SOC and water temperature, if selected. If neither are selected, the line chart will not be visible.
  • Control bar. Select the date (period) to display and control which statistics are visible in the graph.

There are 14 statistics that may be displayed for the visible period:

  • Load. The domestic load.
  • Feed. The amount of electricity exported.
  • Buy. The amount of electricity purchased from the grid.
  • PV. The amount of electricity generated.
  • PV to Battery. The amount of generated electricity stored in the battery.
  • PV to Load. The amount of generated electricity used to satisfy instantaneous load.
  • Battery 2 Load. The amount of stored electricity stored used to satisfy instantaneous load.
  • Grid to Battery. The amount of purchased from the grid for the purpose of storage in the battery.
  • EV Diversion. The amount of generated electricity used to charge EVs
  • Hot Water Diversion. The amount of generated electricity used to heat water
  • State of Charge. The amount of electricity stored in the battery (left axis scale)
  • Hot Water Temperature. The estimated water temperature (right axis scale)
